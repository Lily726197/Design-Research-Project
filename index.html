Welcome to my Design Research Project on Colors!

Colors are very important for UX research and design, which is why I made this research project. In order to have your website accessible to people with disabilities, you need to choose a good color scheme. Here are a few tips:

  1. Don't put light colors on light colors, or dark on dark. Doing so will make the text barely legible.
  2. Provide a high contrast with monochromatic, or one-color, schemes.
  3. Avoid "vibrating colors", or very bright colors placed next to each other, since they can be hard on the eyes.
  4. Choose from different styles, including monochrome, triangular, and square schemes.
