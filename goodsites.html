Good Website Examples

This website uses earthy tones to establish its eco-friendly identity. It is easy on the eyes and relaxing.

Many designers try to avoid yellow as the main color, because it overpowers other colors. However, Banky uses blue as the accent color, making it stand out.

This website uses pink to sell its products to women. The navy blue text provides a great contrast against the pale pink background.
